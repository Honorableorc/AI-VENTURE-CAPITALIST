market sizes, CAGR, competitor names, cost/revenue figures, and risk scores —
rather than summarising them away.

paragraph or a short list — a few sentences each) so that ALL sections fit and
the document finishes properly. It is far better to cover every section
concisely than to write a long Executive Summary and get cut off before the
Investor Pitch. Always finish with the Investor Pitch.
